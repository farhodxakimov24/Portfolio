WEB Design

FRONTEND

Frontend is one of the web technologies, which means "external interface". With the help of this technology, a visual part of web pages that is visible to a person and provides information is created. A person who has studied frontend technologies will be able to realize his various ideas in the form of web pages and programs through his creativity and creative approach.

BACKEND

Backend is anything that runs on the server, i.e. "not in the browser". Or "on a computer connected to a network (usually the Internet) that responds to messages from other computers." It is a set of hardware and software, with the help of which the logic of the site is implemented. That is, this part is not visible to the user.
